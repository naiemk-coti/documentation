# Convert to Public Tokens

The Private Tokens dashboard offers a Portal Out operation that enables you to convert your private tokens back into standard public tokens. To proceed, kindly follow these two steps:

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Authorize the Conversion

* Ensure your private balances are unlocked (see [Step 2](convert-to-public-tokens.md#2-unlock-private-tokens)).
* Select the asset from your Private Tokens list and enter the desired withdrawal amount
* Click the Portal Out button.  .&#x20;

<div align="left" data-with-frame="true"><figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Screenshot 2026-03-24 at 3.42.27 PM.png" alt="Portal Out"><figcaption><p>Portal Out</p></figcaption></figure></div>
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Approve transaction

* Approve and confirm the transaction in MetaMask.
*   The private tokens will be burned, and the transparent public tokens will be returned to your wallet.<br>

{% endstep %}
